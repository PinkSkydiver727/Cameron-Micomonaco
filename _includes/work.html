Virtual Reality Survior Horror

Synthetica allowed the user to manipulate a prop gun to control their weapon in virtual reality with real time tracking. The player navigated an underground laboratory fighting cybernetic monsters while trying to escape

Development Time: 8 months

Team Size: 2 Developers, 3 Designers

Engine: Unity 3D

My Responsibilities:

   - Enemy AI Behaviour

   - Animation Controllers and Sound Cues

   - Assisting with Hardware Integration

   - Assisting with Game Design and Project Managment


Input

A Leap Motion Controller tracked the position of the gun while a Wii Mote provided user input and rotational information

Enemy Behaviour

Enemies were driven by a simple finite state machine. Enemies move between searching and patrolling while the players location is unknown. Once a player is seen an enemy will run to his last known location, if an enemy catches a player they move to an attack state and then idle during the game over animation.

Action-Packed, Gravity-Whacked Space Bowling

Tilt is a quick fun bowling game where the player controls the tilt of the gravity in the alley with their hand! Tilting gravity allows the player to navigate the twisty courses, dodge obsticals, hit jumps for bonuses, or send them plummeting into oblivion.

Development Time: 4 months

Team Size: 2 Developers, 1 Designer, 1 Marketing

Engine: Unity 3D

My Responsibilities:

   - Player stats pertinance between games

   - Touch free menu design

   - Polishing game feel and mechanics

Input

A Leap Motion Controller tracked the position of the players extended finger to point at menu buttons, extending the finger further triggers a button press. While in game the Leap tracks the pitch and yaw of the players hand and applies that information to the tilt of the couse.

OpenGL Asteroids

This simple asteroids game I created using very low level graphics programming. It was made in C++ using only a simple OpenGL extension library(glsh) and mathematics library(glm). I wrote a parser capable of loading .obj files and oranizing vertex data for complex 3D models. I wrote simple shaders for colour and lighting on the models.
